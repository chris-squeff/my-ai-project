# LaunchPad

## Project Overview

An AI-powered campaign planning assistant that helps digital marketers generate complete ad campaign structures across Meta, Google, and TikTok. Users input their budget, goals, and a brief description, and the tool generates keywords, interest targeting suggestions, headlines, and ad copy variationsâ€”reducing campaign setup time from hours to minutes.

### Target Customer
Digital marketers and media buyers managing paid campaigns across multiple platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok), particularly those working with B2B clients in creative industries like music, entertainment, and tech. These are professionals or small agency owners who handle 5-15 campaigns monthly, need to present polished campaign plans to clients, and want to reduce time spent on repetitive campaign setup tasks.

### Value Proposition
Speed and presentation quality. What takes 2-4 hours of manual research and results in a cluttered Google Sheet now takes 5 minutes and produces a polished, visual campaign plan that clients actually want to review. Marketers get AI-powered targeting suggestions across Meta, Google, and TikTok in one place instead of switching between multiple platform dashboards. The tool learns campaign structure patterns and generates professional-looking plans that improve client approval rates and make marketers look more strategic. It's the difference between showing up to a client meeting with a spreadsheet versus a designed strategy document.
